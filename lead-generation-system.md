## 1) Outcomes & Targets
- **Primary outcome:** qualified discovery calls with businesses and high-value individuals.
- **Monthly targets (example, adjust to capacity):**
  - 80–120 new leads
  - 20–30 qualified discovery calls
  - 6–10 proposals sent
  - 3–6 new clients retained

## 2) Ideal Client Profiles (ICPs)
### A) Small Business Owners
- Revenue: $300k–$5M
- Pain points: cash flow, quarterly estimates, bookkeeping issues, tax compliance anxiety
- Services: business tax planning, bookkeeping cleanup, payroll tax support

### B) High-Income Individuals & Families
- Income: $200k+
- Pain points: complex deductions, multi-state income, equity compensation
- Services: personal tax planning, filing, audit defense

### C) Real Estate Investors
- 2+ properties or short-term rentals
- Pain points: cost segregation, depreciation, entity structuring
- Services: planning, entity setup, tax filing, bookkeeping

## 3) Offer & Positioning
- **Core promise:** proactive tax planning to reduce liabilities and remove compliance stress.
- **Signature offer examples:**
  - “Tax Planning Roadmap” (1–2 consultations + written plan)
  - “Year-Round Tax Partner” (monthly/quarterly check-ins)
- **Risk reducer:** free 20–30 minute “Tax Savings Assessment”

## 4) Lead Channels (Prioritize 2–3 to start)
### A) Referrals (Warmest leads)
- Ask existing clients for referrals with a simple prompt.
- Build reciprocal relationships with bookkeepers, attorneys, and realtors.

### B) Local Networking & Community
- Chamber of Commerce, BNI, local business meetups.
- Offer short “Tax Tips” workshops for small businesses.

### C) Content + SEO
- Monthly blog posts targeting local/industry tax queries.
- “Tax Checklist for Small Businesses” PDF as a lead magnet.

### D) Paid Ads (Optional scaling)
- Google Search Ads: “tax planning [city]”, “business tax accountant [city]”
- Retargeting for website visitors.

## 5) Lead Capture System
- **Website landing page** for each ICP (with clear CTA).
- **Lead magnet**: “Quarterly Tax Checklist” PDF.
- **Form**: name, email, business type, revenue range, tax pain point.
- **Calendar booking**: direct scheduling for assessment calls.

## 6) Lead Nurture & Follow-Up
### Email Sequence (Example)
1. Day 0: Deliver lead magnet + intro to firm
2. Day 2: “Top 3 tax deductions you may be missing”
3. Day 5: Case study or client success story
4. Day 8: Invite to free assessment call
5. Day 14: Reminder + testimonials

### SMS Follow-Up (Optional)
- Short, low-pressure reminders with booking link.

## 7) Sales Process
1. **Discovery call** (20–30 min)
2. **Diagnostic + Proposal** (within 48 hrs)
3. **Onboarding** (documents, engagement letter, payment setup)

## 8) Tools & Tech Stack
- CRM: HubSpot, Zoho, or Notion CRM
- Scheduling: Calendly
- Email marketing: Mailchimp, ConvertKit
- Website: WordPress, Webflow, or Squarespace
- Forms: Typeform or Google Forms

## 9) KPIs to Track
- Website traffic & conversion rate
- Cost per lead
- Discovery call show rate
- Proposal acceptance rate
- Client lifetime value

## 10) 30-Day Action Plan (Quick Start)
**Week 1**
- Define ICP + offer
- Create basic landing page
- Set up CRM + calendar

**Week 2**
- Build lead magnet
- Launch email nurture sequence
- Start outreach to 10 referral partners

**Week 3**
- Publish 2 SEO-focused blog posts
- Host 1 local workshop or webinar

**Week 4**
- Review KPIs, adjust messaging
- Start paid ads if budget allows
