# 30-Day Launch Plan (Daily Checklist)

## Week 1: Foundation
**Day 1:** Define ICPs, primary offer, and risk reducer.  
**Day 2:** Draft positioning statement and core messaging.  
**Day 3:** Set up CRM pipeline stages and lead tracker.  
**Day 4:** Create lead magnet outline and landing page wireframe.  
**Day 5:** Build landing page and form fields.  
**Day 6:** Set up scheduling link and confirmation emails.  
**Day 7:** Review everything, fix gaps, and publish landing page.

## Week 2: Assets & Outreach Setup
**Day 8:** Finalize lead magnet PDF.  
**Day 9:** Write 5-email nurture sequence.  
**Day 10:** Add lead magnet delivery automation.  
**Day 11:** Create outreach scripts (referral + partner).  
**Day 12:** Compile list of 25 referral partners.  
**Day 13:** Send first 10 partner outreach messages.  
**Day 14:** Follow up with 5 partners and log in CRM.

## Week 3: Visibility
**Day 15:** Publish blog post #1 targeting local tax query.  
**Day 16:** Publish blog post #2 targeting ICP pain point.  
**Day 17:** Share lead magnet on social channels.  
**Day 18:** Host or schedule a workshop/webinar.  
**Day 19:** Send workshop invites to local list.  
**Day 20:** Attend a networking event.  
**Day 21:** Review leads, update pipeline, and adjust outreach.

## Week 4: Optimization
**Day 22:** Review landing page conversion rate.  
**Day 23:** A/B test CTA or headline.  
**Day 24:** Review email open/click rates.  
**Day 25:** Refine nurture sequence based on results.  
**Day 26:** Follow up with all warm leads.  
**Day 27:** Send 10 new partner outreach messages.  
**Day 28:** Publish a short client success story.  
**Day 29:** Review KPIs and identify bottlenecks.  
**Day 30:** Set goals and plan for the next 30 days.
